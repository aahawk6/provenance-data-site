What we do

Three ways we can help your team

We work across the full data lifecycle — from raw image annotation through to production-ready dataset products. Most engagements start at Tier 1 and grow from there.

Tier 1
Dataset annotation
Bounding box, polygon, and classification annotation for packaging defect detection models. Fast turnaround, multi-pass QA, inter-annotator agreement metrics on every batch.
Tier 2
Dataset curation
Audit and improve existing labeled datasets — coverage gaps, consistency errors, taxonomy alignment, and edge case identification. Written report included.
Tier 3
Dataset products
Pre-labeled, ready-to-train datasets for specific defect classes in beverage and packaging — available for direct licensing. No annotation project required.
Why it matters

Domain knowledge is the difference

Generic annotation services produce generic results. Food and beverage QC has specific tolerances, defect classes, and edge cases that generalist annotators consistently miss.

Domain-specific annotators
Screened and trained on food and beverage QC standards — not generic image labeling. Every annotator passes a defect recognition assessment before working on client data.
Taxonomy development included
We build and document the defect taxonomy before annotation begins — so your model learns what you actually mean by a defect, not a loose approximation.
QA-verified output
Multi-pass quality review on every batch. Inter-annotator agreement metrics provided with each delivery. You see the numbers, not just the labels.
Pilot-first approach
Start with a bounded pilot before committing to a full engagement. Clear deliverables, fixed timeline, defined acceptance criteria. No surprises.
